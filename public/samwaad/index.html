Samwaad

"A good discussion increases the dimensions of everyone who takes part."
Notably, in the country we live in, social concerns impact the lives of the majority in society. It is inescapably necessary and needful to evaluate the problems and start acknowledging them.
Each samwaad is part of a series of 6 group discussions on various exciting topics ranging from social to general that one might want to think about and discuss.

DAY 1

Life without smartphones

Smartphones have become such an integral part of our lives that we cannot imagine a day without them. They have replaced not just a lot of old gadgets, but also outdoor sports, relationships, and books. Join in to be a part of an interesting discussion!
