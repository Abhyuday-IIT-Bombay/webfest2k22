Socio-Weekend features a wide variety of events like talk sessions, workshops, group discussions and panel discussions. The socio-weekend will foreground burning issues that require immediate discourse and action.

Author Talk

Online classes due to the pandemic have led to isolation and unhealthy attachment to social media among many. This has led to a reduced attention span among students. In the minds of the youth, we aim to reinforce the importance of positive and diverse relationships with empathy, reflection, perspective, an open mind and respect through inculcating a reading habit among them.

NFTs: The Next Frontier in Social Impact Space

One of the most recent success stories in the blockchain space in 2021 has been the incredible growth of NFTs. NFTs create a whole new set of opportunities for artists, musicians, developers, and content creators who wish to cut out the middleman and provide their works directly to their customers.
