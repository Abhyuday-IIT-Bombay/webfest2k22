Abhyuday IIT Bombay

Plantation Drive

We all talk about deforestation, climate change but do we actually take actions to prevent it? It's the right time to come together to make our earth greener.

“Trees are green gold.”

Abhyuday in collaboration with Green I Initiative Foundation, Green Yatra and Green India Initiative is organizing 'Grow Green Gold' - a Nationwide Plantation Drive on 22nd January from 9:30 AM to 5:00PM.

Lets pledge together to grow at least one plant no matter wherever you are no matter how much busy you are let's just grow one plant on 22nd January using commonly available items and send a message to society


To be a part of drive: https://forms.gle/mi8zSn5C97bgRZdf8

E-Certificates of participation will be awarded

Submission Form:https://forms.gle/AcqawL81tFkLxfVR8


Guidelines:

Try to find a suitable place for a plantation with proper sunlight and watering facilities. (It’s not about planting a sapling, it’s about caring till it grows to survive on its own)

You can plant a tree individually, in a group(with your friends, in your school/college, etc.) and share photos with us. Go through youtube videos below to know about some domestic ways of planting a tree.
